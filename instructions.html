Robot Study

Instructions

Follow these instructions to complete the study!

  1. Fill in the Intake Questionnaires given by the researcher by email
  2. Do not miss the welcome meeting with the researchers in zoom
  3. In the first week, use the digital workbook at least 3 times per week, and perform at least 2 activities each day. You can use the digital workbooks more than 3 times and perform more than 2 activities. Only at the end of the first week you will be able to move to the robot.
  4. To choose activities from the digital workbook, clock on “Digital Workshops” in the navigation panel.
  5. In the second week use the virtual robot at least 3 times per week, and perform at least 2 activities each day.
  6. To choose activities to do with the robot, click on “Robot Activities” in the navigation panel.
  7. Fill in the Exit Questionnaires given by the researcher by email
  8. Do not miss the exit interview with the researchers in zoom
